About Transnavicom

We create maps of cities and countries.

We sell maps in MapInfo and ArcGIS formats.

We develop ready-made solutions for your business.

We are Transnavicom — a stable and reliable commercial company.

Over 20 years of successful activity in cartography. More than 1,000,000 clients worldwide.

Our core principles since foundation are the highest accuracy, up-to-date data, affordable pricing, and a unified digital database of maps for all countries worldwide. Today, our team consists of thousands of experienced analysts, cartographers, and scientists who work daily for you in both central and the most remote corners of the planet.

We are proud to offer digital maps of 257 countries, over 600,000 cities, and navigation across 58,000,000 km of roads.

Transnavicom takes special pride not only in data for world capitals but also in maps of small developing countries and autonomous regions.

Throughout its history, Transnavicom has kept pace with technological progress and has made numerous technological inventions in cartography and geospatial data processing.

Being an innovative company, Transnavicom always applies advanced technologies and the latest electronics. Over years of successful work, invaluable experience has been accumulated, proprietary methods developed, and unique technical inventions patented.

All this enables us to create and update maps of any territories quickly and with maximum accuracy. Importantly, data processing speed is not our only priority — Transnavicom combines computing power with thorough verification and refinement of maps by professional staff located in offices worldwide. This approach ensures the consideration of historical and linguistic features of each specific region.

For a long time, Transnavicom’s global cartographic database was available only to large corporations, government institutions, military agencies, and intelligence services. Known for high price, accuracy, and unprecedented coverage, Transnavicom maps remained closed and inaccessible to the mass consumer.

Since early 2017, Transnavicom’s board of directors decided to reduce prices and make its maps accessible to small and medium businesses.

Transnavicom is a dynamically developing company. Maps are constantly improved — developed, updated, coverage is expanded daily, and new data layers are added. Productive cooperation and feedback with all data customers and end users have been established.
