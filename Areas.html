We provide easy access to accurate maps of any country or city without lengthy negotiations. You receive from a single source: highest precision maps, fast order fulfillment, easy purchase process, global coverage, continuous updates, and support from Transnavicom’s scientists and engineers in data processing and integration.

Locate yourself, find addresses, plan optimal routes, and discover nearby services such as banks or restaurants easily using various electronic devices and compatible software.

Fleet Management

Effective fleet management is linked to cost control and customer service improvement. Leading fleet management providers and users rely daily on Transnavicom maps to:

  • Track any number of vehicles 24/7
  • Monitor fuel consumption
  • Optimize routes based on distance, time, and other factors
  • Determine speed and direction
  • Calculate travel time and stop durations
  • Receive alerts about emergencies, traffic jams, and other situations
  • Assist in choosing the shortest route to the destination
  • Generate detailed reports on routes, stops, speed, fuel consumption, and more
  • Create detailed driver route sheets
  • Analyze freight transportation routes over any period
  • Maintain archives per vehicle
  • Export data to accounting systems
  • Integrate with logistics systems

Vehicle control and safety features include:

  • Managing factory-installed vehicle alarms
  • Remote blocking of fuel, engine, door/window locks, ignition, etc.
  • Monitoring installed sensors (doors, trunk, windows)
  • Detecting route deviations, unauthorized stops, speeding

Cargo safety includes temperature control of refrigerated goods, unauthorized access detection, container breach alerts, and monitoring vehicle sensors.
